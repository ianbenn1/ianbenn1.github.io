Some of my projects
SpecialDelivery ↗

SpecialDelivery is a drag and drop, copy and paste file transfer program. It can send files to a device on your local network fast. Send photos from your Desktop to your phone in one step.

SpecialDelivery is built using Electron and NodeJS Express

SpecialDelivery isn't quite ready for production yet, but when it is, it'll be here first

Eddies Golf Simulator ↗

Eddies Golf Simulator is a top down animated mobile golf game

Originally developed for a University mobile development course, Eddies Golf Sim is a casual golfing app for people who like customization

Eddies Golf Simulator is made with React Native and Matter.js
